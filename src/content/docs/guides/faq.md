## Installation & Connection

### The launcher won't detect my game installation.

Make sure the game was installed through **Steam** and that Steam is running when you launch TrucklineMP. If detection still fails, open TrucklineMP settings and manually point it to your game directory.

### I get a "version mismatch" error when connecting.

Your game version doesn't match the server's supported version. Check our Discord `#announcements` channel for the currently supported game version. You may need to opt out of Steam betas or roll back an update via Steam's properties menu.

### The game crashes on startup with TrucklineMP.

1. Disable all mods — unsupported mods frequently cause crashes in multiplayer.
2. Verify your game files via Steam (right-click game → Properties → Local Files → Verify).
3. Reinstall TrucklineMP.
4. If the crash persists, post your crash log in `#support` on Discord.

### I can't see other players / they're invisible.

This is usually a desync issue. Try:
- Reconnecting to the server
- Switching to a different server and back
- Restarting the launcher

## Rules & Bans

### I was banned but I didn't do anything wrong.

You can appeal your ban through the official Discord within 7 days of the ban. Go to `#ban-appeals` and follow the pinned format. Provide your account name and any evidence you have.

### Someone rammed me. How do I report them?

Press **F7** in-game to open the report menu and select the player. Attach a screenshot or clip if possible. Alternatively, post in `#player-reports` on Discord with evidence.

### Can I use mods on TrucklineMP?

Only mods that are **visual or audio only** and do not affect physics or gameplay are permitted. Economy mods, map mods, and physics overhauls are not allowed. Always check the mod list in `#allowed-mods` on Discord before using one.

## Account & Profile

### Can I change my username?

Yes, once every **30 days** from your account settings page on the website.

### I forgot my password.

Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page. A reset email will be sent to your registered address.

### Can I have multiple accounts?

No. Each person is permitted one account. Multiple accounts are considered ban evasion if your main account has an active ban, and will result in permanent bans on all accounts.

## Performance

### I'm getting low FPS in multiplayer.

Multiplayer is more demanding than singleplayer. Try:
- Lowering your in-game graphics settings
- Reducing the player density slider in TrucklineMP settings
- Closing background applications

### I'm experiencing high ping / lag.

Connect to a server geographically closer to you. Check your internet connection and make sure no other device is saturating your bandwidth.
