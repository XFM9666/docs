# Profile Settings

Manage how others see you on TrucklineMP. Your profile is your public identity across the platform.

## Accessing Profile Settings

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Select the **Profile** tab
4. Or navigate to `trucklinemp.com/user/me/settings?tab=profile`

---

## Display Name

Your public display name visible to all users.

- **Length:** 3–24 characters
- **Cooldown:** Can be changed once every 30 days
- **Visibility:** Shown on your profile, in VTCs, events, forums, and news feeds

### Tips
- Choose a name that's easy to recognize
- Avoid special characters that are hard to type
- Keep it consistent across platforms

---

## Social Handle

Your unique handle for your profile URL.

- **URL:** `trucklinemp.com/@yourhandle`
- **Length:** 2–15 characters
- **Allowed:** Lowercase letters, numbers, underscores only
- **Cooldown:** Can be changed once every 30 days

### Tips
- Keep it short and memorable
- Match it to your other social media handles
- Avoid numbers that may look like spam

---

## Profile Picture

Your avatar displayed throughout the platform.

### Upload Picture
1. Click **Upload picture**
2. Select an image file (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
3. Maximum file size: 5 MB
4. The image is uploaded and applied immediately

### Sync from Steam
1. Click **Sync from Steam**
2. Your profile picture is refreshed from your Steam account
3. This pulls your current Steam avatar

### Tips
- Use a clear, recognizable image
- Square images work best
- Avoid busy backgrounds
- Make sure your face or logo is visible at small sizes

---

## Social Links

Add links to your social media profiles. These appear on your public profile.

### TikTok
- Enter your full TikTok profile URL
- Example: `https://www.tiktok.com/@yourname`
- Must be a valid TikTok URL

### World of Trucks
- Enter your World of Trucks profile URL
- Example: `https://worldoftrucks.com/en/profile/...`
- Must be a valid World of Trucks URL

### Twitter / X
- Enter your Twitter or X profile URL
- Example: `https://twitter.com/yourhandle` or `https://x.com/yourhandle`
- Must be a valid Twitter/X URL

### Medal
- Enter your Medal profile URL
- Example: `https://medal.tv/u/yourname`
- Must be a valid Medal URL

### URL Validation
- All links must start with `https://`
- Links are validated against the correct domain
- Invalid URLs will show an error message
- If you enter a URL without `https://`, it will be added automatically

---

## Saving Changes

1. Make your changes in the form
2. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom
3. You'll see a success notification
4. Changes are reflected on your profile immediately

---

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't change my name"
- Names can only be changed once every 30 days
- Ensure your name is 3–24 characters
- Check for invalid characters

### "My handle isn't saving"
- Handles must be 2–15 characters
- Only lowercase letters, numbers, and underscores are allowed
- Handles can only be changed once every 30 days

### "My profile picture won't upload"
- Check file format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
- Ensure file size is under 5 MB
- Try a different image file

### "My social links show an error"
- Ensure URLs start with `https://`
- Check that the URL is from the correct platform
- Remove any extra spaces or characters
