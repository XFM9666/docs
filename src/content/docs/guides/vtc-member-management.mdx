# VTC Member Management

The Member Roster allows you to view and manage all members of your VTC, including their roles, join dates, and staff notes.

## Accessing Member Management

1. Navigate to your VTC's management panel
2. Select **Members** from the sidebar
3. You'll see the full member roster

---

## Member Roster Overview

The roster displays:
- **Personnel** — Member avatar, name, WebID, and "YOU" badge for yourself
- **Assigned Roles** — All roles assigned to the member
- **Enlistment Date** — When they joined your VTC
- **Administrative Actions** — Quick action buttons

---

## Searching Members

Use the search bar at the top to find members by:
- **Display name** — Partial or full name match
- **WebID** — Exact or partial ID match

Results update as you type.

---

## Member Actions

Hover over a member row to reveal action buttons:

### Staff Notes
- Click the note icon to open the staff notes modal
- Add private notes about the member
- Notes are only visible to VTC management
- Use for tracking important information, warnings, or achievements

### Edit Roles
- Click the edit icon to open the role assignment modal
- Check/uncheck roles to assign or remove them
- Members can have up to 4 roles
- Changes are saved automatically
- See the current role count (e.g., "2/4 roles")

### Kick Member
- Click the kick icon to remove a member from your VTC
- A confirmation modal appears
- The member loses access immediately
- This action cannot be undone

### Leave VTC (Yourself)
- If you're not the owner, you can leave the VTC
- Click the leave icon next to your own name
- Confirm the action
- You lose access immediately

### Transfer Ownership
- Only available to the VTC owner
- Click the transfer icon next to your own name
- Opens the transfer ownership modal
- Select a new owner from current members
- **Warning:** This action is permanent and irreversible

---

## Role Assignment

### Assigning Roles
1. Click the edit icon next to a member
2. Check the boxes for roles you want to assign
3. Members can have up to 4 roles
4. Changes save automatically

### Removing Roles
1. Open the role assignment modal
2. Uncheck the role you want to remove
3. The change is immediate

### Role Limits
- Each member can hold up to 4 roles
- If a member already has 4 roles, you must remove one before adding another
- The owner role is separate and doesn't count toward the limit

---

## Staff Notes

Staff notes are private annotations visible only to VTC management.

### Adding Notes
1. Click the note icon next to a member
2. Enter your note in the modal
3. Save the note
4. Notes are timestamped and attributed to you

### Use Cases
- Track member achievements
- Document warnings or issues
- Note member preferences
- Record training progress
- Keep contact information

### Privacy
- Notes are only visible to users with management permissions
- Members cannot see their own staff notes
- Notes persist even if the member leaves

---

## Member Roles Display

### Role Badges
- Roles appear as colored badges matching the role's configured color
- Multiple roles are displayed in a row
- The owner badge is always shown for VTC owners

### Unranked Members
- Members without custom roles show as "Unranked"
- Owners always show the Owner badge
- Unranked members have no special permissions

---

## Member Status Indicators

### Owner Badge
- Red badge with shield icon
- Always visible for the VTC owner
- Cannot be removed or transferred without using the transfer feature

### YOU Badge
- Yellow badge shown next to your own name
- Helps you identify yourself in the roster

---

## Best Practices

### Regular Reviews
- Review your member roster periodically
- Remove inactive members
- Update roles as members progress
- Clean up unused staff notes

### Role Management
- Assign roles based on activity and trust
- Use the hierarchy suggested in [VTC Roles & Permissions](/guides/vtc-roles-permissions/)
- Don't assign management permissions unnecessarily

### Staff Notes
- Use notes to track important information
- Keep notes professional and factual
- Review notes before making decisions about members

### Member Engagement
- Welcome new members
- Recognize active members with roles
- Address issues promptly
- Communicate expectations clearly

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see a member"
- Use the search bar to find them
- Check if they've left the VTC
- Verify you have permission to view members

### "I can't assign a role"
- Ensure the member has fewer than 4 roles
- Check that you have permission to manage roles
- Verify the role exists

### "I can't kick a member"
- You cannot kick the VTC owner
- You cannot kick yourself (use Leave instead)
- Ensure you have permission to manage members

### "Staff notes aren't saving"
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have permission to manage members
- Try refreshing the page
