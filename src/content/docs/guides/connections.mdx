# Connections

Connect your external accounts to TrucklineMP for identity verification, account recovery, and profile integration.

## Accessing Connections Settings

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Select the **Connections** tab
4. Or navigate to `trucklinemp.com/user/me/settings?tab=connections`

---

## Available Connections

| Service | Status | Visibility Toggle |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| **Steam** | Permanent (cannot disconnect) | N/A |
| **Discord** | Connect/Disconnect | No |
| **Google** | Connect/Disconnect | No |
| **YouTube** | Connect/Disconnect | Yes |
| **Twitch** | Connect/Disconnect | Yes |

---

## Steam

Your Steam account is permanently linked and cannot be disconnected.

- **Purpose:** Primary identity verification
- **Status:** Shows as "Connected" or "Not connected"
- **Steam ID:** Displayed when connected

This connection is required and cannot be changed.

---

## Discord

Link your Discord account for identity verification and account recovery.

### Connecting
1. Click **Connect** next to Discord
2. You'll be redirected to Discord's authorization page
3. Authorize TrucklineMP to access your account
4. You'll be returned to TrucklineMP
5. Your Discord username is displayed

### Disconnecting
1. Click **Disconnect** next to Discord
2. The connection is immediately removed

### What's Displayed
- Your Discord username is shown when connected
- Used for identity verification and account recovery

---

## Google

Link your Google account for easier sign-in and account recovery.

### Connecting
1. Click **Connect** next to Google
2. You'll be redirected to Google's authorization page
3. Select your Google account
4. Authorize TrucklineMP
5. You'll be returned to TrucklineMP

### Disconnecting
1. Click **Disconnect** next to Google
2. The connection is immediately removed

### What's Displayed
- Shows as "Connected" or "Not connected"
- Used for account recovery and sign-in

---

## YouTube

Link your YouTube channel and control whether it's shown on your profile.

### Connecting
1. Click **Connect** next to YouTube
2. You'll be redirected to Google's authorization page
3. Select the Google account with your YouTube channel
4. Authorize TrucklineMP
5. Your channel name is displayed

### Disconnecting
1. Click **Disconnect** next to YouTube
2. The connection is immediately removed

### Visibility Toggle
When connected, a toggle appears:
- **Show on public profile** — Your YouTube channel is visible to other users
- **Hide from public profile** — Only you can see the connection

Toggle changes are saved automatically.

## Connection Status Indicators

### Success
- After connecting, you'll see a success notification
- The service status updates to show your username
- You may be redirected back with `?status=success` in the URL

### Failure
- If a connection fails, you'll see an error notification
- Try again or contact support if the issue persists

---

## Best Practices

### Account Recovery
- Link at least **two** external accounts (Google + one other)
- This ensures you can recover your account if you lose access

### Privacy
- Review visibility toggles for YouTube and Twitch
- Hide channels you don't want public
- Disconnect unused services

### Security
- Only connect accounts you own
- Keep connected accounts secure
- Disconnect from services you no longer use

---

## Troubleshooting

### "The connection failed"
- Ensure you're signed into the correct account
- Check that you authorized TrucklineMP
- Try again or clear your browser cookies

### "I can't disconnect"
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if the issue persists

### "My channel name isn't showing"
- Refresh the page
- Check that the connection is successful
- Reconnect if necessary

### "The visibility toggle isn't working"
- Ensure you're connected to the service
- Refresh the page and try again
- Contact support if the issue persists
