# VTC Recruitment

Configure how users apply to join your VTC. You can use TrucklineMP's built-in application system or redirect to an external form.

## Accessing Recruitment Settings

1. Navigate to your VTC's management panel
2. Select **Recruitment** from the sidebar
3. You'll see options for application method and form configuration

---

## Application Methods

Choose how applicants will apply to your VTC:

### Truckline Form (Internal)
- Use the built-in application system
- Create custom questions
- Manage applications directly on TrucklineMP
- Applicants see your form on your VTC's page
- You receive notifications for new applications

**Recommended for:** Most VTCs, especially those wanting to manage everything in one place.

### External Link
- Redirect applicants to an external form
- Supports Google Forms, Typeform, or any web form
- Applicants see a confirmation dialog before being redirected
- You manage applications outside TrucklineMP

**Recommended for:** VTCs with existing application systems or complex requirements.

---

## Configuring Internal Forms

### Form Details

**Form Title**
- The title displayed to applicants
- Default: "Apply to [VTC Name]"
- Make it clear and inviting
- Maximum 200 characters

**Introduction / Instructions**
- Markdown-supported text shown at the top of the form
- Explain the application process
- Include requirements or expectations
- Add contact information for questions
- Maximum 5,000 characters

### Creating Questions

Click **Add Question** to create a new question.

#### Question Types

**Short Answer**
- Single-line text input
- Good for: Name, age, experience level
- Applicants enter brief responses

**Paragraph**
- Multi-line text area
- Good for: Detailed explanations, essays
- Applicants can write longer responses

**Dropdown**
- Select from predefined options
- Good for: Multiple choice with many options
- Applicants select one option from a list

**Multiple Choice (Radio)**
- Radio buttons with predefined options
- Good for: Yes/No questions, simple choices
- Applicants select one option

#### Question Settings

**Question Text**
- The question displayed to applicants
- Maximum 1,000 characters
- Make questions clear and specific

**Required**
- Check this box to make the question mandatory
- Applicants must answer required questions
- Uncheck to make optional

**Options (Dropdown/Radio only)**
- Add options for dropdown and radio questions
- Click **Add Option** to add more
- Click the trash icon to remove an option
- Maximum 500 characters per option

### Managing Questions

**Reordering**
- Questions appear in the order you add them
- Remove and re-add to change order

**Editing**
- Click on a question to edit it
- Changes are not saved until you save the entire form

**Deleting**
- Click the trash icon on a question card
- The question is removed immediately
- This cannot be undone

## Configuring External Links

1. Select **External Link** as your application method
2. Enter the full URL to your external form
3. The URL must start with `https://`
4. Save your changes

**What applicants see:**
- A button to apply
- A confirmation dialog warning them they're leaving TrucklineMP
- The external form opens in a new tab

---

## Saving Your Configuration

- Click **Save Configuration** at the bottom right
- All changes are saved together
- You'll see a success toast notification
- Changes take effect immediately

---

## Managing Applications

### Viewing Applications
- Applications appear in your VTC's management panel
- See applicant information and answers
- Filter by status (pending, approved, rejected)

### Reviewing Applications
- Read all answers carefully
- Check applicant profiles for activity
- Use staff notes to track your review process

### Accepting Applications
- Click **Accept** on an application
- The applicant is notified
- They're added to your VTC as a member
- You can assign roles immediately

### Rejecting Applications
- Click **Reject** on an application
- Optionally provide a reason
- The applicant is notified
- They can reapply in the future

---

## Recruitment Blacklist

Prevent specific users from applying to your VTC.

### Adding to Blacklist
1. Go to **Blacklist** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add to Blacklist**
3. Search for the user by username
4. Select the user from results
5. Enter a reason
6. Click **Add to Blacklist**

### Removing from Blacklist
- Click the trash icon next to a blacklisted user
- The user can apply again immediately

### Blacklist Notes
- Blacklisted users cannot submit applications
- The reason is visible to VTC management
- Entries show who added them and when

## Best Practices

### Form Design
- Keep forms concise but comprehensive
- Ask relevant questions only
- Use required fields for essential information
- Provide clear instructions

### Question Ideas
- Why do you want to join our VTC?
- How long have you been playing?
- Do you have a microphone for convoys?
- What is your experience with other VTCs?
- Are you familiar with our rules?

### External Forms
- Use clear, permanent URLs
- Test the form yourself before publishing
- Ensure the form is mobile-friendly
- Provide contact information for support

### Application Review
- Respond to applications promptly
- Provide feedback when rejecting
- Use staff notes to track your decisions
- Be consistent in your evaluation

## Troubleshooting

### "Applicants can't see my form"
- Ensure recruitment is enabled in General Settings
- Check that your form is saved
- Verify the form has at least one question

### "My external link isn't working"
- Ensure the URL starts with `https://`
- Test the link yourself
- Check that the form is publicly accessible

### "I can't save my form"
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check that question text is not empty
- Verify you have permission to manage recruitment

### "An applicant isn't showing up"
- Check if they've submitted the application
- Verify they meet your requirements
- Refresh the page
