# Account Deletion

Learn how to permanently delete your TrucklineMP account and what data is removed.

## Accessing Account Deletion

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Select the **Danger** tab
4. Or navigate to `trucklinemp.com/user/me/settings?tab=danger`

---

## Before You Delete

### Consider These Alternatives
- **Take a break** — You can simply stop using TrucklineMP without deleting
- **Transfer VTC ownership** — If you own a VTC, transfer it first
- **Export your data** — Request a data export before deleting
- **Disconnect accounts** — Remove connections if you plan to re-register

### Important Warnings
- **This action is permanent and irreversible**
- All your data will be permanently removed
- You cannot recover your account after deletion
- Your username and handle may be available for others to use

---

## What Gets Deleted

### Permanently Removed
- Your profile (name, handle, picture, social links)
- All VTC memberships and roles
- Your activity history
- Forum posts and threads
- Event participation records
- Configuration data
- Staff notes about you
- Session data
- Connected account information

### What May Remain
- VTC ownership must be transferred before deletion
- Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics
- Legal records may be kept as required by law

---

## How to Delete Your Account

### Step 1: Initiate Deletion
1. Go to **Settings** > **Danger** tab
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. A confirmation modal appears

### Step 2: Wait for Cooldown
- A 5-second countdown starts
- This prevents accidental deletions
- You cannot proceed until the countdown finishes

### Step 3: Confirm Deletion
1. Type `DELETE` in the confirmation box
2. The delete button becomes active after the cooldown
3. Click **Delete My Account**

### Step 4: Completion
- Your account is immediately deleted
- You are signed out
- You are redirected to the homepage
- A success notification confirms the deletion

---

## After Deletion

### What Happens
- You are signed out of all sessions
- Your profile page returns a 404 error
- Your VTC memberships are removed
- Your forum posts may remain but are anonymized

### Can I Re-Register?
- Yes, you can create a new account with the same email
- Your old data will not be restored
- Your username and handle may be available
- VTC ownership cannot be recovered

## Alternatives to Deletion

### If You're Frustrated
- Contact support via Discord to resolve issues
- Take a break from the platform
- Adjust your privacy settings

### If You Want a Fresh Start
- Change your display name and handle
- Update your profile picture
- Clear your activity by leaving VTCs
- Export your data first

### If You're Concerned About Privacy
- Hide external configurations
- Toggle YouTube/Twitch visibility
- Review and revoke authorized apps
- Disconnect unused connections

---

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't delete my account"
- Ensure you've typed `DELETE` exactly
- Wait for the 5-second cooldown to finish
- Check that you don't own a VTC (transfer ownership first)

### "I own a VTC and can't delete"
- Transfer ownership to another member first
- Go to VTC Settings > Danger Zone > Transfer Ownership
- Once transferred, you can delete your account

### "I deleted my account and want it back"
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed
- You can create a new account
- Contact support if you believe the deletion was a mistake
