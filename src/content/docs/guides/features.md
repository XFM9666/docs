TrucklineMP brings the trucking world to life with a suite of multiplayer features built around the community.

## Real-Time Multiplayer

Drive alongside hundreds of players simultaneously on shared maps. See real players on the road, communicate via CB radio, and experience a living highway network.

## Convoys

Organize or join structured convoys with a designated leader. Convoy tools include:

- **Convoy HUD** — see all members, their positions, and distances in real time
- **Speed sync** — optional limiter to match the leader's speed
- **Voice channel** — dedicated in-game voice for convoy members
- **Route planning** — set a shared route visible to all participants

## Events

TrucklineMP hosts regular community events including:

- **Marathon runs** — long-distance convoys crossing multiple countries
- **Timed challenges** — first to complete a delivery route wins
- **Seasonal events** — special cargos and decorations during holidays
- **Staff-led patrols** — moderator convoys open to the public

Check the `#events` channel on Discord for upcoming schedules.

## Custom Jobs

Take on handcrafted jobs created by the community beyond the base game's economy:

- Oversize loads requiring escort vehicles
- Time-sensitive priority deliveries
- Multi-leg freight contracts across borders

## Player Profiles

Every account has a public profile showing:

- Total distance driven
- Convoy participation history
- Event badges and achievements
- Company affiliation

## Virtual Trucking Companies

Form or join a VTC (Virtual Trucking Company) with other players:

- Custom company tags displayed in-game
- Private convoy channels
- Internal job boards and logistics management
- Company leaderboards

## Moderation Tools

TrucklineMP runs a 24/7 moderation team with tools to keep roads safe:

- In-game report system (`F7`)
- Automated rammer detection
- Replay system for ban evidence
- Dedicated ban appeals process
