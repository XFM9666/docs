# VTC Roles & Permissions

Roles define your VTC's hierarchy and control what members can do. This guide covers creating roles, assigning permissions, and managing your role structure.

## Accessing Role Management

1. Navigate to your VTC's management panel
2. Select **Roles** from the sidebar
3. You'll see all existing roles and their details

---

## Role Limits

| VTC Status | Role Limit |
|-----------|------------|
| Standard | 20 roles |
| Verified | Unlimited |

If you hit the limit, you'll see a warning. Apply for [Verified status](/guides/verified-vtc-program/) to unlock unlimited roles.

---

## Creating Roles

1. Click **Create New Rank** in the top right
2. Fill in the role details:
   - **Role Name** (required, max 50 characters)
   - **Description** (optional)
   - **Color** (choose a color for the role badge)
3. Click **Create Role**

The role is now available to assign to members.

---

## Editing Roles

Click the edit icon on any role card to open the edit modal. You can manage three tabs:

### Details Tab
- **Role Name** — Change the display name
- **Description** — Update the role description
- **Color** — Change the badge color

Changes are saved when you click **Save Changes**.

### Permissions Tab
Configure what members with this role can do. Permissions are grouped by category:

**Management Permissions:**
- `management.vtc.edit` — Edit VTC settings
- `management.vtc.members` — Manage members
- `management.vtc.roles` — Manage roles
- `management.vtc.recruitment` — Manage recruitment
- `management.vtc.news` — Post news
- `management.vtc.events` — Create events

**Other Permissions:**
- Additional permissions may be available based on your VTC's configuration

Use **Select All** to grant all permissions in a category, or **Deselect All** to remove them.

### Users Tab
View all members who have this role:
- See member names and WebIDs
- Remove the role from individual members
- See how many members hold this role

---

## Deleting Roles

1. Click the trash icon on the role card
2. Confirm the deletion in the modal
3. Members with this role will lose it

**Warning:** This action cannot be undone.

---

## Assigning Roles to Members

Members can hold up to **4 roles** simultaneously.

### From the Members Page
1. Go to **Members** in the sidebar
2. Click the edit icon next to a member
3. Check/uncheck roles in the modal
4. Changes are saved automatically

### From the Role Edit Modal
1. Open a role's edit modal
2. Go to the **Users** tab
3. Use the interface to manage who has this role

## Role Hierarchy Best Practices

### Suggested Structure
```
Owner (built-in, cannot be removed)
├── Admin
│   ├── Moderator
│   │   ├── Senior Driver
│   │   └── Driver
```

### Permission Guidelines
- **Owner** — Full control (built-in)
- **Admin** — All management permissions
- **Moderator** — Member management, news, events
- **Senior Driver** — Limited permissions, mentorship role
- **Driver** — No special permissions (default role)

### Tips
- Start simple and add roles as needed
- Use colors to visually distinguish hierarchy
- Write clear descriptions for each role
- Review permissions regularly
- Don't over-complicate your structure

## Role Display

### On Member Profiles
- Roles appear as colored badges
- Members can have up to 4 role badges visible
- The owner badge is always shown for VTC owners

### In the Member Roster
- All assigned roles are listed for each member
- Unranked members show "Unranked" (except owners)
- Role colors match the configured colors

## Troubleshooting

### "I've hit the role limit"
- Standard VTCs are limited to 20 roles
- Delete unused roles or apply for Verified status
- Verified VTCs have unlimited roles

### "Members can't see the management panel"
- Ensure they have the `management.vtc.edit` permission
- Check that the permission is assigned to their role
- Only owners can access certain settings

### "I can't delete a role"
- Ensure no members have this role, or reassign them first
- The owner role cannot be deleted
- You must have permission to manage roles

### "A member lost their role"
- Check if the role was deleted
- Review the activity log for role changes
- Reassign the role from the Members page
