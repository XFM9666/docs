Welcome to TrucklineMP. This guide will get you from zero to driving on the server in a few minutes.

## Requirements

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A legitimate copy of **Euro Truck Simulator 2** or **American Truck Simulator** on Steam
- The game updated to the **latest supported version** (check our Discord for the current version)
- A stable internet connection

## Installation

1. Download the latest **TrucklineMP installer** from the official website.
2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen steps. It will automatically detect your game installation.
3. Launch TrucklineMP from your desktop shortcut or the Start menu — **do not** launch the base game directly.

## Creating an Account

1. Open TrucklineMP and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your email address and choose a username. Your username will be visible to other players.
3. Verify your email and log in.

## Joining a Server

1. From the TrucklineMP launcher, click **Play**.
2. Select a server from the list. Servers are sorted by player count and region.
3. Choose your profile and click **Connect**.

:::tip
If you're new, start on a lower-population server until you're comfortable with multiplayer traffic rules.
:::

## First Steps In-Game

- Drive carefully — other players are real people and collisions have consequences.
- Use your **horn** and **indicators** as you would in real life.
- Respect speed limits and traffic signals on simulation servers.
- Read the [Server Rules](/guides/rules/) before your first session.
