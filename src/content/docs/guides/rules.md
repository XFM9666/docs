---
title: Server Rules
description: Rules that apply to all TrucklineMP players at all times.
---

All players are expected to read and follow these rules. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. Violations can result in kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

## 1. General Conduct

- Treat other players with respect at all times.
- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or threats of any kind are strictly prohibited.
- Impersonating staff members or other players is not allowed.
- Do not exploit bugs or glitches. Report them instead.

## 2. Driving Standards

- **No ramming.** Intentionally colliding with other vehicles is a bannable offense.
- **No blocking.** Do not park on roads, at fuel stations, or in any location that obstructs traffic without good reason.
- **No reckless driving.** Excessive speeding, wrong-way driving, and ignoring traffic signals on simulation servers are all violations.
- Keep a safe following distance — lag and desync can cause sudden stops.

## 3. Convoys

- Follow the convoy leader's instructions at all times.
- Do not overtake the convoy leader unless explicitly permitted.
- Maintain convoy spacing — do not tailgate.
- If you need to leave a convoy, pull over safely before disconnecting.

## 4. Communication

- In-game chat must remain respectful and in English on global servers.
- Do not spam the chat or horn.
- CB radio channels should be used for convoy coordination, not casual chat.

## 5. Usernames & Tags

- Usernames must not contain offensive, discriminatory, or impersonating content.
- Company tags must not reference real companies without authorisation.

## 6. Bans & Appeals

- Bans are issued by moderators based on evidence including screenshots and server logs.
- You may appeal a ban through our official Discord within **7 days** of the ban being issued.
- Ban evasion (creating a new account while banned) results in a permanent ban on all accounts.

:::caution
A first offence for ramming or blocking typically results in a **7-day ban**. Repeat offences escalate to permanent bans.
:::

## Reporting a Player

To report a rule violation, use the **in-game report system** (press `F7`) or submit a report in the `#player-reports` channel on Discord with evidence attached.
